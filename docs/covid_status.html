COVID Testing & Guidance for Schools

CDC, PA Department of Education, and CHOP Recommendations Based on Current COVID Indicators in Philadelphia.

The purpose of this website is to apply COVID-19 school recommendations from trusted sources to the current situation in Philadelphia. To this end, recommended measures and thresholds from the following publications are calculated, and published recommendations based on these indicators are presented.

  • Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
  • Pennsylvania Department of Education (PA DOE) Instructional Model Recommendations
  • Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP) Quick Reference Guide for Reopening Schools

Data notes:

  • Testing data from OpenDataPhilly.
  • 3 most recent days removed from analysis, as Philadelphia city notes recent days are incomplete.
  • The specific dataset used to populate this page appears to report test results based on the day they were taken, and not on the day the results were officially reported. Each day the city adds new data, but many cases are backdated, meaning the most recent days are incomplete and will change.

Notice: This website is a personal project and is not intended for public use. Please do not use this site as a source for reliable information on COVID.
